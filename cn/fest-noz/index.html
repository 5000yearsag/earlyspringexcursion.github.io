Fest Noz,  the breton’s way of life.

Maybe you’ve even never heard about a breton, or maybe you have met a French guy who told you, “I am a breton, from Brittany, you know, nort-west of France.” We, breton, love our local culture, still so much alive in our region Brittany. Next time you come to France, I will be happy to show you!

 

One of the things I miss the most living far away from my homeland Brittany, is definitely dancing in a Fest Noz. It means ‘night festival’ in breton language.

Dancing in cercles, in pairs, with children or grandpa’s

So I decided to organize Fest Noz, here in Dali.
